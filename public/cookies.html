Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and improve your experience.

Essential Cookies

These are required for the site to function. They include authentication tokens (so you stay logged in), session identifiers, and CSRF protection tokens. These cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

We use minimal analytics to understand how the site is used (page views, calculator completions, feature usage). This data is anonymized and aggregated. You can opt out via the cookie banner.

Affiliate Cookies

When you click on product recommendations, the affiliate partner may set a cookie to track the referral. These cookies are governed by the respective partner's privacy policy.

Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences using the cookie banner on first visit, or by clearing cookies in your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may prevent some features from working.
